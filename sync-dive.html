Launch Protocol

  1. Phase 1 — Glyphlink Protocol (M0–3)

    Soft-launch puzzle experience introducing the Glyphbound universe.

  2. Phase 2 — Vyra: Grid Run (M3–6)

    An adrenaline-fueled runner game following Vyra through the neon streets of Neo-Seoul.

  3. Phase 3 — Glyphs of Harmony (M6–10)

    A serene puzzle experience uniting the essence of balance and music in the Glyphbound world.

  4. Phase 4 — N-X: Off Stage (M10–14)

    A visual novel revealing the personal stories of the N-X members beyond the spotlight.

  5. Phase 5 — IONA’s Circuit Dive (M14–20)

    An arcade shooter experience diving deep into IONA’s virtual world of rhythm and danger.

  6. Phase 6 — N-X Stage Rush (M20–24)

    A rhythm-based adventure expanding the N-X mythos and music-fueled world.

  7. Phase 7 — Glyphbound: Neon Anthem (M24–30)

    The centerpiece of the Glyphbound universe, uniting all experiences into one grand narrative.
